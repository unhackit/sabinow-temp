    Criteria for becoming an instructor

    At SabiNow, you can teach what you are good at and impact as many lives as possible. Most topics are acceptable with the exception of a few that are seen as harmful and offensive to our audience. See list below

    Content that negates or goes against the laws of the country

    Inappropriate or dangerous content

    Sexually explicit content

    Content relating to making, handling or using weapons

    Content promoting harm to people or violence

    Racist, Hate speech or discriminatory language

    Content enabling, aiding or abetting illegal or unethical behavior

    Content that is intentionally misleading, fraudulent or deceptive

    We reserve the right to add to and modify this list at any time. If you see a topic that you believe should not be on the platform, please email admin@sabinow.com

    An intending instructor should meet the following requirements:

    Content promoting harm to people or violence

    Racist, Hate speech or discriminatory language

    Content enabling, aiding or abetting illegal or unethical behavior

    Content that is intentionally misleading, fraudulent or deceptive

    An intending instructor should meet the following requirements:

    HD Video

    Good audio quality

    Minimum of 5 modules

    Total video content should be a minimum of 30 minutes

    Free courses must have less than 2 hours of video content

    We advise that you captivate the interest of students with a high level of engagement, very helpful supplemental resources and creative or innovative approaches to presenting the course material.

    Please note that our marketplace model enables you retain all the rights to your content when you upload your course on our platform. It only grants us a license as hosts and we make it available to our users, we advertise your course on our platform and on third party services.
